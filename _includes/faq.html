Frequently asked questions

What kind of test do we use?

We have partnered with MicroGenDX to offer FDA-approved Reverse Transcriptase PCR (rRT-PCR) assay for the specific detection of the SARS-CoV-2 virus. The assay validation exceeds the minimum recommendations put forth by the FDA guidance policies.

How do you administer the test?

You will recieve a kit that includes easy-to use sputum collection device. Simply collect and ship it back at your convinience.

Is this test covered by insurance?

In many cases we are able to work with your insurance providers to reduce and often eliminate the costs of the testing.

How often should we test our employees?

Most our clients elect for seasonal testing, once every three months.
